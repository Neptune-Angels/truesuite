# TrueSuite Brand

## Identity
**TrueSuite** = a coordinated set of dead-simple SMB tools. Each product solves one painful, recurring problem for small business owners without the bloat of enterprise software.

## Voice
- Clear over clever
- Direct, no jargon
- Treat the SMB owner like a busy adult, not a marketing target
- No dark patterns, no fake urgency, no "limited time"

## Products
- **TrueQR** — dynamic QR codes + scan analytics. Static QR is free, dynamic is paid.
- **TruePage** — landing page builder. One-page sites that load fast and convert.
- **TrueBook** — appointment booking. Calendar, reminders, no-show protection.

## Naming
Always "True\<X\>" — single word, capital T, capital next letter. The suite is "TrueSuite" (one word).

TrueQR sets the initial baseline. As TruePage/TrueBook ship, extract shared design tokens into `packages/ui`.
